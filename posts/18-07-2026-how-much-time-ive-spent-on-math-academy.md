# How much time I've spent on Math Academy

A while ago I wrote that [maths is now the best part of my day](/maths-is-now-the-best-part-of-my-day.html). I'd like to share with others how much time this actually took me, so they can calculate their own trajectories.

| Course | Status | Active days | Total XP | XP / active day | Active days / week |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Foundations I | Completed | 138 | 3,357 | 24.3 | 4.6 |
| Foundations II | Completed | 246 | 6,853 | 27.9 | 5.0 |
| Foundations III | In progress | 199 | 3,901 | 19.6 | 4.0 |

An "active day" is any day I completed at least one task. Math Academy says that 1 XP == 1 minute, but I've seen it's ofen more like 1 XP == 3 minutes. As a rule of thumb, I've spent about ~1 hour on every active day.
