# I built an interactive unit circle

I got tired of memorising trigonometry by rote so I built an [interactive unit circle](/unit-circle.html). This allows me to drag a point around the circle and watch sine, cosine and tangent update in real time. 

[![Interactive unit circle](/static/images/08-03-2026-interactive-unit-circle/unit-circle.png)](/unit-circle.html)

The tutorial button will walk you through how all six trig functions are derived from the unit circle.

My hope is that by internalising the unit circle, I won't have to remmeber anything by rote anymore.
